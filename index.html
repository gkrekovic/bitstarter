Mighty Knob

Choosing the right VST synth and tweaking its' parameters has always been tricky and time consuming. Mighty Knob is here to make things easier!

‹ ›

For musicians

 Save time and keep focused on your musical ideas. Discover a lot of exciting synths which are specifically suggested to fit your needs. Unleash your creativaty using this totally different experience of synth programming.

For VST developers

 The best way to promote your synth is to let users hear it. Besides generating patches, Mighty Knob also suggests synths to users and lead them to your web shop or a product page. Contact us and join in!

Spread the word

 Collaboration with more VST developers will make Mighty Knob even mightier. So, don't hesitate to spread the word!
